Projects

I have done two projects - a minor project and a major project.
  1. IoT Based smart street light using light sensor :

    The prototype is designed in such a way that it helps us in preventing manual switch on/off street lights. Based on the detection of movement of person/vehicle lights switch on/off automatically and also get adjusted to the surroundings light intensity.

  2. IoT based women safety device :

    Basically it is portable device which is connected to a registered mobile number through GPS and internet. whenever switch is pressed it automatically updates the woman's location via SMS and an email is sent to the registered email-Id providing the location link and stating that she is in danger and needs urgent help.
